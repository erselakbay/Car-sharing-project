What We Do

In this project, we aimed to provide infrastructure so that you, dear students, can find comfortable and safe vehicles on their way to school. Let's Road allows you to be a travel companion with your friends who go to school from your close circle in appropriate time periods!

Suitable Time Intervals

Thanks to short time intervals, you can contact the person closest to you at the right time.

Fast Support

Thanks to more than one employee in the support team, we provide you with the fastest support as soon as possible.

Reliable

Non-university logins using student emails are blocked. In this way, students can communicate with other users under a secure infrastructure.

Socialization

Thanks to this transportation service, students can socialize with their friends from different departments and faculties.

Contact Us




If you encounter any problems while using the project, or if you have suggestions for the improvement of this website, please contact us. Our support team will get back to you as soon as possible.
